Everything you need. Nothing you don't.

Built to stay out of the way until you actually need it.

◫

Workspaces

Keep contexts separate — one workspace per team, client, or project. Switch from a dropdown in the title bar; people, notes, exports, and imports are all scoped to the active workspace.

⚡

Global shortcut

Press ⌃⌘⌥Space from anywhere — your IDE, browser, Slack — and a note form appears instantly. Or use the + Note button inside the app. No context switching.

🎯

Sentiment tagging

Every note is tagged positive, neutral, or negative. Colour-coded at a glance so patterns emerge without reading every entry.

📅

Two views, one picture

A global timeline for the full story, and a per-person feed grouped by month for when you're writing a specific review. Full-text search across all notes.

⇅

Export & import

Export notes filtered by date range — copy JSON or save to file. Import a previous export to restore or migrate your data between machines.

✦

AI Summaries

Select a person and a date range, pick a purpose preset (review prep, retro, etc.), and generate a structured summary from your notes. Powered by OpenRouter — bring your own API key and any model you like.

☁︎

GitHub Sync

Back up your notes to any GitHub repository with one click. Push and pull the active workspace as a JSON file, or enable auto-sync to run silently in the background every 5 minutes to 24 hours. Your data, your repo.

Three steps, two seconds.

1

Trigger the overlay

Hit ⌃⌘⌥Space. A floating panel appears centred on your screen — no mouse required.

2

Pick, tag, write

Search for the team member by name, select a sentiment with one click, type your note. Press ⌘↵ to save.

3

Summarize when it counts

Open the dashboard at review time. Jump to a person's feed, hit ✦ Summarize, pick a date range and a purpose — get an AI-generated summary ready to copy or save as a note.
